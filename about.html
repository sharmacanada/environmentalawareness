About Our Project

Understanding the mission, objectives, and impact of environmental awareness through social media advocacy

Project Mission

Environmental Awareness Through Social Media is a capstone project that aims to utilize the potential of digital channels to empower sustainable practices and make people environmentally responsible.

This capstone project demonstrates how digital media can be both informational and empowering, helping unite individual environmental action with larger sustainability goals. Through strategic use of social media platforms, we aim to create measurable impact in environmental awareness and behavior change.

Our Strategic Objectives

1

Conduct awareness through distribution of easy-to-read and visual materials about recycling, climate change, and sustainable living

2

Access younger audiences who are most active online through Instagram and TikTok

3

Engage users in interactive programs like challenges, live-action, and online communities

4

Motivate sustainable behavior and spearhead policy change

5

Connect individual behavior to global environmental issues
